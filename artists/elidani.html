ELI & DANI

Eli & Dani is a DJ and Producer duo from Israel that consists of members Eliav and Daniel. The duo are one of the most successful electronic acts in Israel and are starting to gain fame internationally as well. Eli & Dani are known for their Melodic Techno style with uplifting and emotional sound, alongside high-quality low-end that brings out energy and drive in their performances.
