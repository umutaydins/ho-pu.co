JAMES HARCOURT

Born and raised in the UK, James Harcourt brings a unique blend of influences to his production work, thanks to his diverse heritage. Immersed in the dynamic London club scene, he grasped the profound impact of electronic music early on. Since 2021, his distinctive sound and dancefloor-focused tracks have found their way into the DJ sets of industry giants such as Tale Of Us, Sasha, Adriatique, Marino Canal, and John Digweed, with his reputation continuing to expand.
