UN:SAID

Ary Sya is an emerging talent in the melodic house and indie dance scene, known for his deep connection to sound frequencies. Starting his musical journey eight years ago on the French Riviera, Ary quickly gained international recognition with tracks like “5 AM” and “My Silence” which have caught the attention of renowned artists such as Adriatique, Solomun, and Rufus Du Sol.
